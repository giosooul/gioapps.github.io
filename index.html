Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Beer Styles app handles information. Beer Styles currently does not collect, transmit, or share personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Beer Styles does not collect personal information or usage analytics.

  • No account data: no name, email, or user profile data is requested or stored.
  • No tracking data: no analytics, activity logs, or behavioral tracking are collected.
  • No device identifiers: no advertising ID or similar identifiers are collected.

2. Local Data and Settings

Beer Styles works with local resources and local app storage only.

  • Information shown in the app is loaded locally.
  • Your app preferences and settings are saved locally on your device.
  • These local settings are not sent to us.

3. Sharing of Information

Because Beer Styles does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to sell, rent, or share with third parties.

4. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not retain personal data on our servers. Local settings remain on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.

5. Security

Local settings are stored using your device storage mechanisms.

7. Third-Party Services

Beer Styles currently does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs to collect user data.

8. Changes to This Policy

If Beer Styles changes how data is handled in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated with a new effective date.
